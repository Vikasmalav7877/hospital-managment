Health Care Solutions

Orthopedic

Deals with bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and recovery after fractures or surgeries, improving mobility, strength, posture, flexibility, and overall well-being.

Neurological

Helps patients with stroke, spinal cord injury, Parkinson’s, multiple sclerosis, etc., by restoring movement, reducing pain, improving strength, balance, and independence.

Cardiopulmonary

Focuses on heart and lung conditions like asthma, COPD, post-heart surgery recovery, improving breathing capacity, endurance, circulation, fitness, and overall quality of life.

Pediatric

For children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, or muscular problems, enhancing coordination, posture, strength, motor skills, confidence, and overall functional independence.

Geriatric

Supports elderly patients with arthritis, balance issues, osteoporosis, and age-related problems, improving mobility, reducing pain, preventing falls, increasing independence, and enhancing daily living.

Sports

Prevents and treats sports injuries, enhances performance, and speeds up recovery, restoring strength, flexibility, stamina, agility, confidence, and overall athletic excellence.

Women’s Health

Focuses on pregnancy care, postnatal recovery, pelvic floor issues, improving core strength, posture, flexibility, comfort, confidence, circulation, breathing, mobility, relaxation, and overall women’s health.

Community

Provides rehab and preventive care in community and rural areas, promoting awareness, accessibility, mobility, independence, strength, wellness, education, and healthier living.

Rehabilitation

Restores independence after major injuries, surgeries, or chronic illnesses, improving mobility, confidence, strength, balance, function, recovery, resilience, and overall quality of life.
